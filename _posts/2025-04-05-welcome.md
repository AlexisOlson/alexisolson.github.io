#### SQL
```sql
SELECT
    customer_id,
    first_name,
    last_name,
    SUM(order_total) AS total_spent
FROM
    orders
JOIN
    customers ON orders.customer_id = customers.id
WHERE
    order_date >= '2024-01-01'
GROUP BY
    customer_id, first_name, last_name
ORDER BY
    total_spent DESC
LIMIT 10;
```
